Here's a time line of Muhammad Ali's life

  • 1942 - Born in Louisville, Kentucky
  • 1959 -started his boxing career at kentucky and won National Golde Gloves Light Heavtweight Champion
  • 1960 - won all of his fights easily at the Olympics. In the final he defeated three-time European champion Zbigniew Pietrzykowski and won the light-heavyweight gold medal
  • 1964 - shocks the world by dethroning world heavyweight champ Sonny Liston in a seventh-round technical knockout, he retained that for three years
  • 1974 - won the Sportsman of the year, Sports illustrated
  • 1979 - Receives Honorary Doctorate of Humane Letters, Texas Southern University
  • 1979 - Street named after him in his home town, Louisville Kentucky
  • 1981 - Retired from boxing
  • 1985 - Recognised for long, meritorious service, World Boxing Association
  • 1987 - Elected to Boxing Hall of Fame
  • 1990 - Inducted into International Boxing Hall of Fame
  • 2016 - dies at the age of 74.
